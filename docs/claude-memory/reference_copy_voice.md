## Two Voices

- **Ryan (Warm & Encouraging):** Rooting for you. Always honest. Never preachy.
- **Byron (Bold, Witty & Snarky):** Funny, not mean. Audacious, not cruel.

## Scenario Copy (insight + follow-up)

### Goal Setting - "I want to plan a trip to Japan"
- Ryan: "Japan in December? Love that. Rs22k/month and you are actually there."
- Ryan follow-up: "Let's figure out exactly what needs to change to make this happen."
- Byron: "Checked your spending. Checked flights to Tokyo. Can we not just do sushi at a desi restaurant?"
- Byron follow-up: "Right. Japan is not going to fund itself. Here is what we are doing."

### 18 Swiggy Orders This Month
- Ryan: "18 orders this month. Intentional or did Swiggy just win?"
- Ryan follow-up: "Want to cap your food delivery budget at Rs5k next month and see what happens?"
- Byron: "Saw the 18 orders. Did not see 18 reasons why you could not cook."
- Byron follow-up: "Want to put a Rs5k ceiling on delivery and see if your kitchen finally gets a second chance?"

### Goal Behind Pace
- Ryan: "One rough month does not break the plan. Rs6k extra and you are back."
- Ryan follow-up: "Can we trim Rs2k off dining and get December back on track?"
- Byron: "At this pace Japan is a 2027 problem."
- Byron follow-up: "Cut Rs2k off dining or Japan becomes a postcard someone else sends you."

### Goal Ahead of Pace
- Ryan: "You are ahead of pace. Whatever you did this month, it is working."
- Ryan follow-up: "Want to lock in a little more while you are on a roll? Even Rs500 extra pushes the date closer."
- Byron: "Your future self just sent a thank you note. From Japan."
- Byron follow-up: "You are on a streak. Want to lock in Rs500 more before present you ruins it for future you?"

### Large Dining Spend (Rs5k+)
- Ryan: "Rs7k on dining this week. Special occasion or just a big week?"
- Ryan follow-up: "Want to set a dining budget for next week so it does not sneak up again?"
- Byron: "Rs7k on dining? I hope someone proposed at this dinner."
- Byron follow-up: "Want to cap dining at Rs4k next week so the only thing proposed is a savings plan?"

### Autopay / Subscription Creep
- Ryan: "Rs1,800/month on autopilot. Worth a quick look?"
- Ryan follow-up: "Want to see the full list and kill the ones you do not actually use?"
- Byron: "Rs6k on autopay? These subscriptions have been billing you longer than most of your situationships."
- Byron follow-up: "Want to see which ones you are actually using and which ones are just vibing on your salary?"

### Shopping Spike (Myntra / Amazon haul)
- Ryan: "Big shopping week. Totally fine if it was intentional. Was it?"
- Ryan follow-up: "Want to cap your shopping budget at Rs3k a month and see if the urge survives?"
- Byron: "Rs8k on Myntra in one weekend. Your closet is full. Your savings is not."
- Byron follow-up: "Want to set a shopping budget so the next sale does not take us both by surprise?"

### Weekend Spending Higher Than Weekdays
- Ryan: "Spending tends to spike on weekends. Want to keep an eye on it together?"
- Ryan follow-up: "Want to set a weekend budget so Saturday you have a number to work with?"
- Byron: "Monday to Friday you are fine. Saturday hits and suddenly money is just a concept."
- Byron follow-up: "Want to set a weekend limit so Monday you stops waking up to financial crime scenes?"
